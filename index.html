Corporate overview

Verneldos Global Services Ltd was created to bridge real gaps across agricultural value chains, financial access, and commercial trade. Our business model combines practical field experience with responsible corporate governance to deliver measurable value to clients, communities, and partners.

Who we are

We are an indigenous Nigerian company operating with integrity, reliability, and customer-centric execution. Our mission is to empower communities and businesses through quality agricultural inputs, dependable digital financial services, and efficient supply-chain solutions.

  • Incorporated under CAMA 2020 and registered with CAC.
  • Strategic focus on food security, value addition, and inclusive commerce.
  • Committed to sustainability, regulatory compliance, and long-term partnerships.

Vision, mission & values

  • Vision: To become a recognised conglomerate for excellence in agriculture, financial inclusion, and sustainable development.
  • Mission: To deliver high-quality agricultural products, reliable digital financial services, and seamless supply-chain solutions.
  • Core values: Integrity, customer-centricity, agility, reliability, and excellence.

Core business portfolios

Our three strategic pillars are built to create value at every stage of the market chain, from rural agricultural production to commercial distribution.

Agro-business & agricultural value chain

Crop and livestock production, processing, distribution, and the supply of certified agricultural inputs.

Agency banking & financial inclusion

POS operations, account opening support, account verification, and micro-financial linkage services for underserved communities.

Manufacturer representation & general trade

Distribution partnerships for consumer and industrial goods, paired with efficient wholesale and logistics support.
